New book claims Heinrich Himmler was murdered

by Arthur R. Butz

It appears to be just a coincidence that, just as the reports came out, of forgery of the documents used by author Martin Allen to argue that Himmler was murdered, a book was about to be published in Germany arguing the same without dependence on those documents.

Thus the Arndt Verlag had to delay the release of the new book Himmlers Tod until some of the confusion could be resolved. The book is now available.

The author is American freelance writer Joseph Bellinger, who discussed his project in a recent interview.

28 August 2005
